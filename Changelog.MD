## Versioning Policy

- **Major (x.0)**: Breaking changes to layer contracts or Skill Interface Contract
- **Minor (1.x)**: New layers, protocols, references (backward compatible)
- **Patch (1.0.x)**: Documentation, clarity, typo fixes
